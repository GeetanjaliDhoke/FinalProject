How does donation work ?

Online cancer donation campaigns that seek small monetary donations to help an individual or family cover expensive cancer treatments and chemotherapy is called cancer donation.

We collects the amounts from people and gives it to our cancer patients to help them in the treatment procedure also we gives medicines to them in the low amount, with that they could afford the medicines and treatment.
